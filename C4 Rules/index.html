Connect 4 Rules

Connect 4

  • Players take turns dropping red or yellow checkers into one of 7 columns into the lowest available row.
  • Continue dropping the checkers until one player has a row of 4 of his or her checkers in a row.
  • The row can be up and down (vertical), across (horizontal), or diagonal.
  • The first player to make a row wins the game.
  • A full board with no rows of 4 is a tie.
